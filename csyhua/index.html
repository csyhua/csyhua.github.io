Brief Biography: Dr.Yu Hua was Postdoc Research Associate in McGill University in 2009 and Postdoc Research Fellow in University of Nebraska-Lincoln in 2010-2011. He was Research Assistant in The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2006, and Intern Student in Bell Labs Research China in 2003. He obtained his B.E and Ph.D degrees from Wuhan University, respectively in 2001 and 2005. His research interests include file systems, cloud storage systems, non-volatile memory, big data analytics, etc. His papers have been published in major conferences and journals, including USENIX FAST, USENIX ATC, ACM SoCC, SC, HPDC, ICDCS, MSST. He serves for multiple international conferences, including ASPLOS, SOSP, USENIX ATC, RTSS, ICDCS, IPDPS, MSST, DATE. He is the senior member of ACM, IEEE and CCF, and the member of USENIX. He has been appointed as the Distinguished Speaker of ACM and CCF. He releases multiple source codes in GitHub for public use.
